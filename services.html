Our Services

At Naledi Motors, we offer much more than just vehicles. We provide a complete automotive experience — from buying your car to keeping it on the road for years to come.

New Vehicle Sales

We are an authorised dealer for Toyota, Ford, Volkswagen, Nissan, and many more leading brands. Browse our full range of brand-new vehicles, available for immediate delivery or factory order. All new vehicles come with a manufacturer's warranty.

Pre-Owned / Used Vehicles

Looking for great value? Our pre-owned inventory is carefully selected and fully inspected. Every used vehicle comes with a comprehensive multi-point inspection report so you know exactly what you are buying. We offer vehicles to suit all budgets.

Vehicle Finance & Insurance

Our finance team works with all major banks in Botswana to find you the best loan terms and interest rates. We can also assist you with comprehensive vehicle insurance through our trusted insurance partners — all in one place.

Workshop & Servicing

Our state-of-the-art workshop is staffed by manufacturer-trained technicians. We offer scheduled servicing, oil changes, brake inspections, tyre replacements, and full mechanical repairs for all makes and models. Genuine parts are always used.

Trade-In Programme

Want to upgrade your current vehicle? Bring it in for a free, no-obligation valuation. We offer fair market prices for trade-ins and will apply the value directly to your new or used vehicle purchase.

Licensing & Registration

We handle all the paperwork! Our administrative team will assist you with vehicle registration, licensing, and transfer of ownership documents — so you can focus on the excitement of your new purchase.

Fleet & Corporate Sales

Does your business need multiple vehicles? Our dedicated fleet sales team offers special corporate pricing, customised fleet management solutions, and flexible delivery schedules to keep your business moving.
