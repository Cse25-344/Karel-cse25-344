About Naledi Motors

Who We Are

Naledi Motors is one of Botswana's most trusted and well-established car dealerships. Located in the heart of Gaborone, we have been serving the people of Botswana with quality vehicles and exceptional customer service for many years.

The name Naledi means "Star" in Setswana — and just like a star, we aim to shine brightly in everything we do: from the quality of our vehicles to the warmth of our customer service.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to make car ownership accessible and enjoyable for every Motswana. We believe that buying a car should be an exciting experience — not a stressful one.

We work hard to offer transparent pricing, honest advice, and flexible finance options so that you can drive away in the car of your dreams without the headache.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We are honest and transparent in all our dealings. No hidden fees. No surprises.

Customer First

Every decision we make is guided by what is best for our customers.

Community

We are proud to be a Botswana business that gives back to our local community.
